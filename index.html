A few words about me

My research interests include: Key-value stores, flash storage, distributed storage systems, Docker container, and DRAM.

I am working on the cutting-edge technologies for storage systems. Specifically, my research focuses on tailoring storage systems for the scalability, availability, and growing data demands of modern high-end computing applications.

To this end, I explore new technologies and storage media that can simplify and dramatically reduce the cost of storage management, and create new solutions that push the limits of today’s data storage capabilities, and explore extreme performance storage systems that can store data reliably for decades.
